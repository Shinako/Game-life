Design

This is a local shoe production company which edits,reveiws,tests,and design new attarctive retractiable heel footware

why are we special?

The M.A.T fashion retractable heel is created using a series of flexable fabrics layered together in order to create a shoe sole that can flex and change to the position and posture of your feet.

How it works

This shoe will have two layers of leather and a layer of memory foam in the center, while the bottom is lined with plastic.the shoe itself will be water proof and obtain the ability to link with your phone. This will allow you to control the heel via your phone and if your phone dies there will also be a button hidden in velcro at the bottom of the shoe.
